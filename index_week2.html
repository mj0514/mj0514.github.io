Week2 Reading Assignment

As We May Think (1945), Vannevar Bush

Bush talks about the evolution of science, technology, and machine and with such evolvements how they impact our lives. In past, with lack of technologies, scientists and people were facing difficulties with well storing data and resources. With advancements of technologies such as development of photography, recordings, films, scientists and people can process things faster and more efficiently which improve the quality of way of living. The technologies are continuously improving and they will become more compact and reliable.

Long Live The Web (2010), Tim Berners-Lee

Berners talks about the benefits of web and preservation of it. With the web, the companies and individuals can share information and resources with public without any inhibition. With such technology, people can have more freedom of speech in websites and possible to share variety of ideas with anyone. However, with such high freedom, we always need to be careful that anything can be on website even if it is inappropriate or false which can be very vulnerable. Therefore, there always need some rules and confirmation but the boundary of limitation is always difficult to decide.
